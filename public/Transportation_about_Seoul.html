ソウルの公共交通機関(public transportation in Seoul)

Welcome to our "ソウルの公共交通機関" page, where we provide essential information on how to navigate Seoul's public transportation system like a local. From the subway network to buses and taxis, this guide is your key to exploring the city with ease. Discover the routes, schedules, and payment methods that will help you reach your desired destinations efficiently. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning traveler, let us empower you with the knowledge to make the most of Seoul's extensive public transportation options.

Airport to Seoul

Airport Express Train (AREX) : The Airport Express Train is a convenient and fast way to reach Seoul. Follow the signs to the AREX station located within the airport terminal. There are two options:

  • Express Train: Takes approximately 43 minutes to Seoul Station, with limited stops.
  • All-Stop Train: Takes approximately 58 minutes, stopping at multiple stations along the way.

Airport Limousine Bus : There are various airport limousine bus routes that connect Incheon Airport to different areas of Seoul. Look for the bus ticket counters or information desks in the arrival hall to purchase a ticket. The buses are comfortable and provide luggage storage space. The travel time depends on the destination and traffic conditions.

T-Money Card

The T-money card is a rechargeable transportation card used in Seoul, Korea. It is a convenient and widely accepted payment method for various modes of public transportation, including the subway, buses, and even taxis.you can purchase one at designated locations, such as subway stations, convenience stores, or through vending machines.

Seoul Metro

Seoul has an extensive and efficient subway system that is often the preferred mode of transportation for both locals and tourists. It provides signs and announcements in both Korean and English. You can easily navigate the subway using route maps, smartphone apps (Kakao Metro), or by asking for assistance at the stations.

Ticket Vending Machines : If you decide not to purchase T-Money, it is fine! You can just use the Ticket Vending Machinesnear the entrance gates. These machines allow you to purchase single journey tickets by selecting your destination station. The machines accept cash and sometimes debit/credit cards.
